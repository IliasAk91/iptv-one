Privacy Policy – IPTV One

Last updated: January 2026

IPTV One respects your privacy. This application does not collect, store, or process any personal data.

Data Collection

IPTV One does not collect personal information, usage data, analytics, tracking data, or identifiers of any kind.

Data Storage

IPTV One uses iCloud to sync your playlists, profiles, favorites, and preferences across your devices. This data is stored securely in your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to the developer. All data synced through iCloud remains under your control and can be managed through your iCloud settings.

Content

IPTV One does not provide, host, or distribute any media content. All playlists, media sources, and streams are added and managed locally by the user on their own device.

Accounts

No user accounts or registrations are required. IPTV One uses your Apple ID for iCloud sync, which is managed entirely by Apple.

Subscriptions

IPTV One offers optional premium subscriptions through Apple's in-app purchase system. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple, and we do not have access to your payment information, billing details, or credit card information. Subscription status is managed by Apple and synced across your devices through your Apple ID.

Third-Party Services

IPTV One does not integrate analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services. The app only uses Apple's iCloud service for data synchronization, which is subject to Apple's privacy policies.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: iptvonebugs@gmail.com
